"The Algorithm" was built by Decifer Studio, founded by Alia ElKattan and Lujain Ibrahim, through the generous support of The Mozilla Foundation's Creative Media Award.

While research on how recommender systems function is prevalent in computer science and related fields, the public's understanding of “The Algorithm” is extremely limited. This project aims to change that.

"The Algorithm" is a part of our studio's greater mission of dymistifying emerging technologies like algorithms and articial intelligence, explaining both how they work and do not work, to the general public. We believe an informed public can more meaningfully participate in the development and regulation of these technologies, and that we can collectively shape our technological futures.
